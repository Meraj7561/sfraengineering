About Company

SFRA Engineering LLP is a multidisciplinary civil engineering firm specializing in detailed design and execution planning for infrastructure projects across India.

With core expertise in building structures, bridges, railways, and highways, we deliver engineering excellence through innovation, precision, and professional commitment.

Backed by a team of highly qualified engineers and CAD professionals, we provide services ranging from structural design, project management, and CAD drafting to site coordination.

Our goal is to provide end-to-end civil engineering solutions that are technically sound, cost-effective, and aligned with the latest industry standards.
