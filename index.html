About Company

SFRA Engineering LLP is a multidisciplinary civil engineering firm specializing in detailed design and execution planning for infrastructure projects across India.

With core expertise in building structures, bridges, railways, and highways, we deliver engineering excellence through innovation, precision, and professional commitment.

Backed by a team of highly qualified engineers and CAD professionals, we provide services ranging from structural design, project management, and CAD drafting to site coordination.

Our goal is to provide end-to-end civil engineering solutions that are technically sound, cost-effective, and aligned with the latest industry standards.

Our Team

ARUN KUMAR

Sr. Structure Engineer

Arun Kumar is a Senior Structural Design Engineer with over 15 years of professional experience, having worked with leading MNCs on diverse infrastructure and building projects. He holds a B.Tech in Civil Engineering and an M.Tech in Structural Engineering, and is currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Structural Engineering. He specializes in detailed structural design and DPR (Detailed Project Report) preparation for high-rise RCC buildings, bridges (railway and highway), railway structures, FOBs, PEB industrial projects, and steel structures. He is actively involved in structural analysis, detailed engineering, and design optimization, ensuring compliance with IS Codes, IRC guidelines, and Railway standards. With extensive experience across RCC and steel structures, Arun Kumar has contributed to highway, railway, industrial, and infrastructure projects, delivering safe, efficient, and sustainable engineering solutions.
